Mutual Fund Taxation-2020

Tax on income from regular dividend

In Union Budget 2020, Finance Ministry changed the tax policy in dividends. In prior policy, fund houses are responsible to pay Dividend Distribution Tax or DDT. But now investors need to pay taxes on the income from dividends. From 1st April 2020, the income from dividends will be added to your income and tax will depend on your income slabs.
