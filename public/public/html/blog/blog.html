Finance and Money Management in 2022

We, at myplexus.com believe the financial intermediation and personal finance industry in India is going through the fastest evolutionary stage and in the coming days this is only going to become more hectic.

Product life cycles will compress. Variations in performances will manifest itself more frequently. Risk evaluation will take centre-stage for marking the success or failure of every financial product or solution. Unbiased, user defined statistical tools will be the bedrock for understanding performance and evaluating efficacy of funds and fund managers in delivering returns quantum, providing it consistently and most specifically managing its various risk factors within manageable levels.
