Rank , Quartile, Decile

The dark days of the scheme was the second half of 17-18 FY and the first half year of 19-20 FY where the fund had made a low of rank 10 and a second quartile position. Most other funds would have been happy to see that position for any period in its life time. But not this fund. It quickly realigned itself to changed market conditions and did a splendid rebound. So much so, that on an absolute basis, the fund has generated stellar no. 1 positions in rank, quartile and decile. In fact, one of the hallmarks of the fund has been in re-strategizing itself and quickly cover lost ground, in spite of very steep addition to its AAUM. Commendable.

The fund manager(s) obviously do not suffer from any stock or sectoral romance, perhaps with the exception of ICICI Bank. And justifiably so.
As the fund grew in size, the lumpiness in holdings did not happen as is so normal in so many other schemes. The total of the top consistent holdings fell from a high of 37% of the total portfolio to just 24% in recent times. March '20 saw the lowest portfolio concentration of scrips with the top 15 contributing just 21% Healthy focus on scrips to ensure maximum bang for the buck and a very actively managed portfolio is evident across the time periods. There is no evident sector bias in the portfolio construction. The focus is on pharmaceuticals but overall it is a diversified portfolio.

Myplexus.com Feedback

The Mirae Asset Emerging Business fund has shown fantastic resilience to ride over a very rough market situation and yet remain ahead of its peers in most parameters. Based on the statistical evidence on hand we are very confident the fund will continue to be a top end performer in the coming times too. The portfolio is adequately diversified and the fund manager follows a full investment strategy as far as we have seen and thus we are not concerned about market timing and the pitfalls of such endeavours. Overall an outstanding performance which might be damped by the volatility issue but we see noreason why the performance will not be sustained.
